Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to MyHome ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Nexus Technologies LLC ("Company") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our MyHome mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use MyHome, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, email address, and profile photo
  • Property Listings: Property details, descriptions, photos, and location data you provide
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged through the App
  • Payment Information: Transaction records (processed securely through third-party payment providers)

Automatically Collected Information

When you access the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location (with your permission) for property search features
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on the App
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display and manage property listings
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Process payments

2. Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Develop new features
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

3. Communication

  • Send notifications about your listings
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send promotional messages (with your consent)

4. Safety and Security

  • Verify user identities
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our terms of service

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors (Telebirr, CBE Birr, Stripe)
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Push notification services (Firebase)

Other Users

  • Your public profile information
  • Property listing details
  • Contact information (phone number) when you choose to share

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password storage (hashing)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  2. Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  3. Deletion - Request deletion of your account and data
  4. Portability - Request your data in a portable format
  5. Opt-out - Opt out of promotional communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@myhome.nexusapps.com

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain certain information for legal, business, or safety purposes.

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained as required by law

Children's Privacy

MyHome is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

Location Data

The App uses location services to:

  • Show properties near you
  • Display property locations on maps
  • Provide distance estimates

You can disable location services in your device settings. Some features may not work properly without location access.

Third-Party Services

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services:

  • OpenStreetMap (maps)
  • Firebase (analytics, notifications)
  • Payment providers

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the App
  • Sending a notification to your registered email
  • Displaying a notice when you open the App
